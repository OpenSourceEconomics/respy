Explanations
============

Explanations help you deepen your knowledge on the concepts and topics behind
**respy**. This section provides an theoretical outline of the class of economic models
that can be implemented using **respy** and points you towards further literature to
deepen your understanding.

    <h2>Modeling Framework</h2>
     
The explanations below showcase what type of economic models **respy** can be
applied to using the example of Keane and Wolpin (1997, :cite:`Keane.1997`), which 
constitutes not only a seminal contribution to labor economic and structural econometric 
literature, but also served as one of the foundation stones in **respy**'s design and 
construction. The guides can therefore serve as inspiration for specifying your own 
model with **respy**.
